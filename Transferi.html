The winter transfer window opened on 1 January 2022 and closes at 23:00 GMT on 31 January 2022.

Clubs will have until midnight on 31 January to complete the necessary information/document uploads on FIFA's Transfer Matching System (TMS).

All transfer fees are undisclosed unless otherwise stated.
